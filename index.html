Ninja Cat

You have Nine Lives. Make them count.

"Ninja Cat" is a game created by Australian PICO-8 game developer cubee, originally released for the PICO-8 fantasy console in early 2021.

In 2023, development for "Ninja Cat Remewstered" began, a remaster of the original PICO-8 version targeted for both Desktop and Android.

The game was briefly a target for speedrunners, who managed to complete the game in less than 3 minutes, an incredible time considering cubee's own best time of around 5:17.

Gameplay

Ninja Cat can throw shuriken.
He runs and jumps at high speed; he's not particularly sneaky despite being a ninja.
He can also use his headband as a grappling hook, making him move even faster!

Obstacles

  • Red Ninjas will run and jump to chase Ninja Cat.
  • Shielded Ninjas walk slowly and blindly toward Ninja Cat, but are invulnerable to his attacks.
  • Searchlights will see and kill Ninja Cat, unless he can find a way to dash past them quickly.

Development

Progress on "Ninja Cat" was posted on the Lexaloffle BBS, where a few members posted feedback on the game. The 1.0 release happened after 1 or 2 weeks of developmnt time.
